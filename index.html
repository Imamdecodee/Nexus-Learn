Ilm Nexus was founded on May 10th, 2025, with a clear vision to empower youths through digital skills, knowledge sharing, and community-driven initiatives. Since its establishment, Ilm Nexus has recorded significant milestones in training, mobilization, and student engagement across multiple campuses and states. Organizational Restructuring
As part of a strategic move to ensure sustainability, efficiency, and national expansion, Ilm Nexus has undergone a formal organizational restructuring. The organization now operates through the following teams: Ilm Nexus Headquarters Team
